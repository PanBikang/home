About

Hi, I'm Bikang — a computer science Ph.D. student at ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Prof. Ye Shi (石野).

My research centers on Embodied AI — particularly world action models and reinforcement post-training for building agents that perceive, plan, and act in complex environments.

I'm broadly interested in policy learning, vision-language foundation models, and optimization. If any of this resonates, I'd love to hear from you — drop me an email to discuss or explore collaboration.
